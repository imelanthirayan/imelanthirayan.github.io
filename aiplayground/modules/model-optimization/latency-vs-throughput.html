Key Takeaways

  • • Latency means the waiting time for one request.
  • • Throughput means total requests handled over time.
  • • Low latency is important for interactive apps.
  • • High throughput is important for large-scale processing.
  • • Optimization often balances both.

Summary

Latency is about speed for one user. Throughput is about how much work the system can handle overall. Good AI systems usually balance both.
