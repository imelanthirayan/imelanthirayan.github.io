Why reflection helps

First answer, or a reviewed answer?

A quick review before finishing can make the final response much clearer.

Without reflection
The agent gives the first answer.
It may be too vague or incomplete.
With reflection
The agent reviews and improves the answer.
The response can be clearer and more useful.
Reflection limits

Reflection helps, but has limits

Reflection improves an answer, but it does not make it perfect.

Reflection can improve clarity.
Reflection can catch missing details.
Reflection can reduce obvious mistakes.
It does not guarantee the answer is always correct.
Important outputs may still need human review.
🔑
Key takeaways
  • • Reflection is a review step.
  • • The agent checks its own output.
  • • Reflection can improve quality.
  • • It is useful for complex or important tasks.
  • • Human review may still be needed.
