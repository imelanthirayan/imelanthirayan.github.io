Key takeaways
  • • Human in the Loop adds review before action.
  • • It is useful for important or risky tasks.
  • • The AI can prepare work, but humans can approve it.
  • • It improves trust and safety.
  • • Not every task needs human review.

Summary

Human in the Loop keeps people involved when AI actions are important, risky, or need judgment. The AI can help prepare the work, but the human gives the final approval.
