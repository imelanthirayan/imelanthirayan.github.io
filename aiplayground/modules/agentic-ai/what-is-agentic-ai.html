Agentic AI
🟡 Intermediate · 6–8 min

What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI is when an AI system works toward a goal through multiple steps. It can plan, use tools, observe results, and adjust what it does next.

AI Agent vs Agentic AI

What is the real difference?

Both are helpful. The difference is how far they go and how much they adjust along the way.

AI Agent
Completes a task
May use tools
May use memory
Often follows a direct flow
Agentic AI
Works toward a larger goal
Plans multiple steps
Observes results
Adjusts the next action
Can continue until the task is complete
🔑
Key takeaways
  • • Agentic AI is goal-driven.
  • • It often works through multiple steps.
  • • It can plan before acting.
  • • It can observe results and adjust.
  • • It is useful for complex tasks, not simple one-step answers.

Summary

Agentic AI is useful when a task is too complex for a single response. It helps AI systems plan, act, observe, and adjust until the goal is completed.
