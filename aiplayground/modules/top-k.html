Top-K Search

Top-K means returning only the best K results instead of showing everything, we filter to what matters most.

Known words use preset semantic positions. Unknown words get a character-based estimate.

🎓 Beginner Tips 2 tips
K=1 means "give me the single best match", useful when you want exactly one, highly relevant result.
Top-K is used in two different places: in vector search to limit results, and in LLM generation to limit which words can be chosen next.
💡
Key Takeaway

Top-K keeps the best few results instead of returning everything this is how search engines and AI assistants stay fast and relevant.
