The Famous Analogy
king − man + woman ≈ queen
vector arithmetic on word meanings
➖ king − man removes the "male" concept, leaving "royalty".
➕ + woman adds the "female" concept back.
🎯 The nearest word to the result vector is "queen".
🎓 Tips 2 tips
The king−man+woman=queen result wasn't programmed, it emerged automatically from seeing millions of sentences during training.
This experiment made word vectors famous in 2013 (Word2Vec paper). It showed AI models were capturing real semantic relationships.
💡
Key Takeaway

Vectors can capture relationships, not just individual objects. By combining vectors, AI models can represent new ideas and compare them with existing concepts.
