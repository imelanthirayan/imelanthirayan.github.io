🎓 Tips 2 tips
Keyword search is exact, "running shoes" won't match "jogging trainers" even though they mean the same thing.
Semantic search uses embeddings, it finds meaning, not letters. That's why modern AI search feels smarter than old keyword search.
💡
Key Takeaway

Keyword search looks for the same words. Semantic search tries to find similar meaning even when the exact word isn't there.
