RLHF

RLHF means Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback. It helps improve a model by using human preferences about which responses are better.

Why Human Feedback Helps

People know what "better" feels like

A model can be correct yet still unhelpful. Human feedback adds the human sense of what makes a good answer.

More Helpful
Humans can prefer answers that are clearer and more useful.
Safer
Humans can reject risky or harmful responses.
Better Tone
Humans can prefer polite and appropriate responses.
Better Alignment
The model becomes closer to what users expect.
Before vs After Feedback

From correct to genuinely helpful

Before feedback, an answer can be right but flat. After feedback, the model leans toward answers people actually like.

Before RLHF
The model may answer correctly but not helpfully.
"RAG combines retrieval and generation."
After RLHF
The model gives a clearer, more useful response.
"RAG helps AI answer using documents instead of guessing."
How It Works

Feedback becomes better behavior

Three simple steps turn human choices into a more helpful model.

1
The model gives a few possible responses.
2
Humans compare them and pick the better one.
3
The model learns to prefer that kind of answer.
🔑
Key takeaways
  • • RLHF uses human feedback.
  • • Humans compare or rate model responses.
  • • Feedback helps the model prefer better answers.
  • • RLHF can improve helpfulness, tone, and safety.
  • • RLHF does not make the model perfect.
