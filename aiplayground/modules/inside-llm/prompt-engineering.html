🎓 Tips 3 tips
Be specific about format, "List 3 bullet points" gives better results than "Tell me about it".
Giving one example of the output you want (few-shot prompting) dramatically improves results.
Shorter isn't always better, a clear 50-word prompt beats a vague 5-word one every time.
💡
Key Takeaway

Clear prompts usually lead to clearer and more useful AI responses.

Adding a task, audience, format, context, and tone turns a vague request into a precise instruction the model can act on.
