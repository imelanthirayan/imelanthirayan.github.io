Tips

  • → Position still matters even without left-to-right scanning, transformers add positional encodings so "dog bites man" ≠ "man bites dog".
  • → Every word attends to every other word at once, unlike older RNN models which read one word at a time. This parallelism makes transformers fast to train.
💡
Key Takeaway

A Transformer does not read text like a simple left-to-right scanner. It looks across the sentence to understand how words relate.

A pronoun like "it" is understood by checking which nouns appeared earlier the model sees the whole sentence at once.
