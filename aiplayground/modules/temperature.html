This is a simple learning demo using predefined responses, not a real AI model.

🎓 Beginner Tips 2 tips
Use temperature 0 for facts, code, and calculations, you want deterministic, repeatable answers.
Temperature above 1.0 can produce incoherent text. Most production apps stay between 0.2 and 0.9.
💡
Key Takeaway

Lower temperature makes responses more predictable. Higher temperature makes responses more creative but less controlled.

Most AI products use a temperature around 0.7 a balance between consistency and variety.
